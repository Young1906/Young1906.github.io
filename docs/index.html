Noise constrastive estimation

Sep 23, 2023
Documenting my note why reading Noise-contrastive estimation paper. Notation $p_d(x)$ true probability density function (p.d.f) of data. $p_n(x)$ p.d.f of noise generating distribution. $r(x) = \frac{1}{1+\exp(-x)}$ sigmoid function. Threorem I $\tilde{J}$ attains a maximum at $f(.) = \log p_d(.)$.
